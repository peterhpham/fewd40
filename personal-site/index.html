A little about me, mostly about the work.

Designer and Digital Production

Currently I work at Square as a production designer. Traditionally trained in graphic design at the Academy of Art University, SF. With a background in print and packaging design, my passion is building brand systems and scalable design. Recently I've shifted towards digital and love the ability to build and prototype ideas quickly.

Pressman and Business Owner

In addition to working as a designer and digital production artist, I also have founded and operate a boutique print shop called The Weekend Press. This small business specializes in custom printing and specialty finishes techniques such as letterpress, foil stamping and die-cutting. After a long day staring at screens and working on a computer, it's great to get hands on, touch paper and create tangible printed goods.
